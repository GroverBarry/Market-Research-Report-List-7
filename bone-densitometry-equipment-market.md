<p><strong>The Bone Densitometry Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Bone Densitometer</li><li>X-ray Bone Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Densitometry Equipment Market comprises two main types: Ultrasound Bone Densitometers and X-ray Bone Densitometers. Ultrasound Bone Densitometers utilize high-frequency sound waves to assess bone density, offering a portable, radiation-free option ideal for screening. In contrast, X-ray Bone Densitometers, specifically dual-energy X-ray absorptiometry (DXA), provide more precise measurements by using low-dose X-rays to evaluate bone density and diagnose conditions like osteoporosis. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring bone health and guiding treatment choices.</p></p>
