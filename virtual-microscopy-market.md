<p><p>The virtual microscopy market encompasses various imaging techniques, notably Brightfield and Fluorescence microscopy. Brightfield microscopy utilizes transmitted light to visualize specimens, ideal for stained tissue samples, providing clear images for routine diagnostics. In contrast, Fluorescence microscopy employs specific wavelengths of light to excite fluorescent dyes, allowing for the observation of dynamic processes and specific cellular components. Both methods enhance diagnostic accuracy and research capabilities, appealing to pathologists and researchers in fields like oncology and molecular biology.</p></p>
