<p><strong>The Rigid PU Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid polyurethane (PU) catalyst market is segmented into non-reactive amine catalysts and reactive amine catalysts. Non-reactive amine catalysts promote the curing process without participating in the chemical reaction, enhancing efficiency and allowing for better control over foam characteristics. In contrast, reactive amine catalysts actively engage in the reaction, facilitating cross-linking and influencing the final material properties. Both types are essential in the production of rigid PU foams used in insulation, automotive, and construction applications.</p></p>
