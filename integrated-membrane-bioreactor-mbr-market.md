The market is categorized into three main types: Hollow Fiber Membrane, which offers high surface area and compact design; Flat Sheet Membrane, known for ease of maintenance and lower fouling; and Multi-Tubular Membrane, which provides robustness and thermal resistance. Each type has distinct advantages based on application, efficiency, and operational costs, catering to diverse industrial and municipal needs in wastewater management.</p></p>
