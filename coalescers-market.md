<p><strong>The Coalescers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Coalescers</li><li>Electrostatic Coalescers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coalescers are devices used to separate two immiscible liquids, often in applications like oil-water separation. Mechanical coalescers utilize a physical mechanism, such as gravity or filters, to aggregate droplets, enhancing separation efficiency. In contrast, electrostatic coalescers employ an electric field to induce droplet collision and coalescence, increasing the separation rate. Both types serve critical roles in industries like petrochemicals, water treatment, and oil refining, ensuring product quality and compliance with environmental regulations. Their market growth is driven by industrial demands for effective separation technologies.</p></p>
