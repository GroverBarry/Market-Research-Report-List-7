Self-propelled units enhance efficiency by moving independently, whereas tractor-mounted collectors attach to tractors for large-scale dust management. Trailed dust collectors follow towing vehicles, allowing for easy transport across larger areas. Fixed collectors are permanently installed in specific locations for continuous dust control in industrial settings.</p></p>
