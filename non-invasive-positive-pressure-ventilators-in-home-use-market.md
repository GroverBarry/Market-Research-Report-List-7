<p><strong>The Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (In-Home Use) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (NPPVs) for in-home use are primarily categorized into two types: single-level ventilators and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators deliver a constant airflow, providing steady pressure to assist with breathing. They are often used for patients with mild respiratory conditions. Bilevel ventilators, on the other hand, offer two distinct pressure levels—one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation—making them suitable for patients with more complex respiratory needs, including those with obstructive sleep apnea or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD).</p></p>
