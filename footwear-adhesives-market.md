<p><p>The footwear adhesives market comprises various types tailored to specific bonding needs. Solvent-based adhesives offer strong bonds and quick drying, suitable for industrial applications. Water-based adhesives are eco-friendly, easier to handle, and ideal for lightweight footwear. Radiation-cured adhesives, like UV or electron beam, provide fast curing and high performance, enhancing durability. Hot melt adhesives offer instant bonding upon cooling and are widely used in high-speed production processes, making them versatile for diverse footwear manufacturing.</p></p>
