<p><ul><li>Active Vibration Isolation Workstations</li><li>Passive Vibration Isolation Workstations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration isolation workstations market comprises two main types: active and passive vibration isolation workstations. Active vibration isolation workstations utilize sensors and actuators to detect and counteract vibrations dynamically, providing a highly effective solution for sensitive equipment. In contrast, passive vibration isolation workstations rely on materials and structural designs to absorb and dissipate vibrations without active intervention. Both types are essential in industries such as manufacturing, research, and healthcare, where precision and stability are critical for optimal performance.</p></p>
