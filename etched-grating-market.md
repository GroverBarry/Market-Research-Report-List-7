<p><strong>The Etched Grating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Grating</li><li>Reflection Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The etched grating market comprises two primary types: transmission and reflection gratings. Transmission gratings are designed to allow light to pass through, dispersing it based on wavelength, making them suitable for applications like spectroscopy and optical communication. In contrast, reflection gratings reflect light, providing efficient wavelength separation and are often used in instruments like spectrometers and lasers. Both types are essential in various industries, including telecommunications, astronomy, and material characterization, driving innovation and growth in optical technologies.</p></p>
