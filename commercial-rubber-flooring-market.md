<p><p>The commercial rubber flooring market is primarily divided into two types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber flooring, derived from latex, offers excellent durability, resilience, and eco-friendliness, making it suitable for various commercial spaces. Synthetic rubber flooring, on the other hand, is engineered from polymers like styrene-butadiene and ethylene-propylene, providing enhanced versatility, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness. Both types cater to diverse applications, including gyms, schools, and healthcare facilities, ensuring a balance of performance and aesthetics in high-traffic environments.</p></p>
