<p><p>RC suppressors and snubbers are vital components used in electrical circuits to protect sensitive devices from voltage spikes and transients. An RC suppressor typically includes a resistor and capacitor in series or parallel configurations, effectively dampening voltage transients and improving circuit reliability.
