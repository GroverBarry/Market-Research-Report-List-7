<p><strong>The Airport Tugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Towbars</li><li>Towbarless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Airport tugs are used to tow and push aircraft on the ground. There are two main types of airport tugs - conventional towbars and towbarless tugs.</p><p>Conventional towbars require a towbar to be connected to the front landing gear of the aircraft for towing. This method is commonly used for smaller aircraft.</p><p>Towbarless tugs, on the other hand, lift the aircraft by its nose gear without the need for a towbar. This method is more efficient and commonly used for larger aircraft. Both types of tugs play a crucial role in the efficient operation of airports.</p></p>
