<p><ul><li>EPDM (Synthetic Rubber) Cables</li><li>Polypropylene (Plastic) Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical submersible pump cable market primarily comprises two types: EPDM (ethylene propylene diene monomer) synthetic rubber cables and polypropylene plastic cables. EPDM cables are renowned for their excellent heat, ozone, and water resistance, making them ideal for deep well applications. In contrast, polypropylene cables are lightweight, flexible, and resistant to corrosion, suiting environments with challenging chemical exposures. Both types ensure reliable performance in various submersible pump applications, contributing to their growing demand in oil, gas, and water management sectors.</p></p>
