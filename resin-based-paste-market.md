<p><p>The resin-based paste market is primarily segmented into epoxy resin-based and methacrylate resin-based types. Epoxy resin pastes are known for their strong adhesion, durability, and resistance to chemicals, making them suitable for aerospace, automotive, and construction applications. In contrast, methacrylate resin pastes offer rapid curing and flexibility, ideal for applications in dental products, coatings, and adhesives. Each type caters to specific industry needs, reflecting varying performance characteristics and application requirements.</p></p>
