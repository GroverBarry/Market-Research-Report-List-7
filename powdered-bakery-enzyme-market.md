<p><p>The powdered bakery enzyme market encompasses various types, each serving distinct functions in the baking process. Bakery protease enzymes enhance dough strength and improve the texture of baked products by breaking down proteins. Bakery carbohydrase enzymes, including amylases, facilitate carbohydrate breakdown, aiding fermentation and texture improvement. Bakery lipase enzymes contribute to fat digestion and improve dough elasticity and shelf life. Together, these enzymes enhance product quality, extend freshness, and optimize baking processes, catering to the evolving demands of the bakery industry.</p></p>
