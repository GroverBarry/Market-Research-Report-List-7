<p><p>The sunroof glazing market comprises three main types: single glazing, double glazing, and triple low-E glazing. Single glazing consists of a single layer of glass, providing basic insulation and light entry. Double glazing features two glass layers with a space in between, enhancing thermal insulation and reducing noise. Triple low-E glazing takes this a step further with three glass layers and low-emissivity coatings, offering superior energy efficiency, UV protection, and temperature control, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
