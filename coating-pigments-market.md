<p><ul><li>Organic Coating Pigments</li><li>Inorganic Coating Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coating pigments market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic pigments. Organic coating pigments are carbon-based and offer vibrant colors, high tinting strength, and excellent transparency, making them popular in artistic and decorative applications. In contrast, inorganic coating pigments are mineral-based, known for their durability, opacity, and UV stability, commonly used in industrial and protective coatings. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing aesthetic appeal and providing functional properties in various coatings.</p></p>
