<p><p>Medical grade protective mask filter material market is segmented into two main types: PP nonwoven and PTFE nonwoven. PP nonwoven filters are made from polypropylene fibers, offering excellent breathability and particle filtration. PTFE nonwoven filters are made from polytetrafluoroethylene fibers, providing superior chemical resistance and durability. Both materials are commonly used in the production of high-quality protective masks for medical use, offering different benefits and meeting various filtration requirements in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
