<p><p>Aircraft and Aerospace Aluminum Casting Components market types include Die Casting, Permanent Molding Casting, and Others. Die casting involves forcing molten aluminum into a mold cavity under high pressure, producing complex shapes with high accuracy. Permanent molding casting utilizes a reusable mold that is heated before pouring in the molten aluminum. The Others category encompasses various casting methods not classified under Die Casting or Permanent Molding Casting, such as sand casting or investment casting. Each type offers unique advantages and is commonly used in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
