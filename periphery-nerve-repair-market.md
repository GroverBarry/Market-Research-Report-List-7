<p><p>The peripheral nerve repair market consists of three primary types: nerve conduits, nerve wraps, and nerve grafts. Nerve conduits are artificial tubes that guide regenerating nerve fibers, promoting healing. Nerve wraps are protective barriers that encase damaged nerves, supporting recovery and minimizing scar tissue formation. Nerve grafts involve taking tissue from another part of the body to bridge gaps in damaged nerves, thus restoring function. Together, these solutions aim to enhance nerve regeneration and improve patient outcomes following peripheral nerve injuries.</p></p>
