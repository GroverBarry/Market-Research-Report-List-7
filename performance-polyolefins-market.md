<p><p>Performance polyolefins include various types such as LDPE, LLDPE, HDPE, and others. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and good chemical resistance, suitable for packaging applications. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) provides high tensile strength and puncture resistance, often used in agriculture and construction films. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers strong moisture barrier properties, used in bottles and containers. Other types of performance polyolefins include UHMWPE (Ultra-High-Molecular-Weight Polyethylene) and MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene), each with specific properties and applications.</p></p>
