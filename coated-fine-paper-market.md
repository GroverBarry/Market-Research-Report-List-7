<p><p>The coated fine paper market consists primarily of gloss-coated and dull-coated paper types. Gloss-coated paper features a shiny finish that enhances color vibrancy and is ideal for high-quality prints, such as brochures and magazines. In contrast, dull-coated paper has a non-reflective surface, offering a balanced matte finish that reduces glare, making it suitable for text-heavy documents and professional reports. Both types are widely used in printing due to their ability to deliver superior image quality and tactile appeal.</p></p>
