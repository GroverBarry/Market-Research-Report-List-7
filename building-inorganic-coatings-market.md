<p><ul><li>Water-soluble Coatings</li><li>Solvent Coatings</li><li>Emulsion Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inorganic coatings market is categorized into three main types: water-soluble coatings, solvent coatings, and emulsion coatings. Water-soluble coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering eco-friendly applications and easy cleanup. Solvent coatings contain organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and durability but posing environmental concerns. Emulsion coatings combine water and solvent components, balancing performance and environmental impact. Each type serves various applications, allowing for tailored solutions in industries such as construction, automotive, and manufacturing, enhancing surface protection and aesthetics.</p></p>
