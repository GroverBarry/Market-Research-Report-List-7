<p><p>Nonwoven fabrics for medical protective clothing are categorized into several types based on their structure and performance. SMS (Spunbond-Meltblown-Spunbond) nonwoven offers a combination of softness, strength, and liquid repellency. SMMS (Spunbond-Meltblown-Meltblown-Spunbond) adds an extra meltblown layer for enhanced barrier protection. SMMMS (Spunbond-Meltblown-Meltblown-Meltblown-Spunbond) further increases filtration and liquid resistance. Additionally, "Other" nonwovens may include various innovative materials and technologies, catering to specific needs in medical applications, ensuring safety and comfort for healthcare professionals.</p></p>
