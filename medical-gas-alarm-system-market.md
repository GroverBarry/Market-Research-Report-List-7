<p><strong>The Medical Gas Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Master Alarm</li><li>Area Alarm</li><li>Local Alarm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Gas Alarm System market comprises three main types: Master Alarm, Area Alarm, and Local Alarm. </p><p>Master Alarms centralize monitoring by overseeing multiple areas and alerting staff to system-wide issues. Area Alarms focus on specific zones, providing localized alerts for gas supply or pressure problems. Local Alarms are designed for immediate, on-site notifications, often activated when a particular gas level deviates from safe standards. These systems are vital in healthcare settings to ensure patient safety and uninterrupted operation of medical gas systems.</p></p>
