<p><strong>The Automotive Gear Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Gear Mineral Fluid Lubricants market comprises three main types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from saturated hydrocarbons, offering high stability and good low-temperature performance, making them popular for gear oils. Naphthenic lubricants, composed of cyclic hydrocarbons, provide excellent lubricity and thermal stability, commonly used in high-pressure applications. Aromatic lubricants, containing unsaturated hydrocarbons, offer superior viscosity and anti-wear properties but are less favored due to environmental concerns. Each type serves specific performance and application requirements in automotive gear systems.</p></p>
