<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Fuel Injector</li><li>Direct Fuel Injector</li><li>Sequential Fuel Injector</li><li>Port Fuel Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive fuel delivery and injection systems market encompasses various technologies designed to optimize engine performance and efficiency. Throttle body fuel injectors mix air and fuel at the throttle body, while direct fuel injectors inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber for precise atomization and improved power. Sequential fuel injectors operate individually to deliver fuel at specific engine cycles, enhancing efficiency. Port fuel injectors spray fuel into the intake manifold, improving combustion characteristics and overall engine performance. Each type plays a critical role in modern vehicle systems.</p></p>
