<p><p>The metal packaging coatings market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and powder coatings. Liquid coatings, commonly used for their ease of application and versatility, are ideal for complex shapes and thinner layers, providing excellent adhesion and finish. In contrast, powder coatings, applied as a dry powder and then cured under heat, offer durability and resistance to scratching and chemicals. Both types cater to various industries, including food and beverage, cosmetics, and automotive, ensuring product protection and aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
