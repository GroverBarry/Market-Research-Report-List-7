<p><p>Styrene Isoprene Styrene (SIS) is a type of thermoplastic elastomer with two common market types: linear and radial. Linear SIS has a linear structure with a single styrene block in the middle, making it suitable for applications requiring high elasticity and flexibility. Radial SIS has a radial structure with multiple styrene blocks surrounding the isoprene core, offering better heat resistance and improved processing properties. Both types of SIS are widely used in industries such as automotive, adhesives, and footwear for their unique blend of properties.</p></p>
