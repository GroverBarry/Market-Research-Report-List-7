<p><strong>The Mechanical Micropipette Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical micropipette market is segmented into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel micropipettes are designed for precise volume delivery in individual samples, commonly used in applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, multi-channel micropipettes feature multiple channels that allow simultaneous handling of several samples, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput tasks, such as ELISA assays or PCR setups. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs, with users selecting based on their workflow requirements and the volume of samples being processed.</p></p>
