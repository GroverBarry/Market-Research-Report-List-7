<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Magnetic Sensing Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance Effect)</li><li>TMR (Tunnel Magnetoresistance Effect)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnetic sensing chips market is defined by three primary technologies: AMR, GMR, and TMR. AMR chips utilize the anisotropic magnetoresistance effect for accurate magnetic field detection, commonly found in automotive and consumer electronics. GMR chips leverage giant magnetoresistance for enhanced sensitivity and are widely used in data storage and reading applications. TMR technology employs tunnel magnetoresistance, offering even higher sensitivity and lower power consumption, making it ideal for advanced applications like IoT and automotive systems.</p></p>
