<p><p>High-capacity fluid-bed dryers are essential in efficiently drying bulk materials in various industries. Static fluid-bed dryers utilize a fixed bed of particles where heated air flows through, enabling even moisture removal. In contrast, vibrating fluid-bed dryers incorporate vibrations to enhance particle movement, promoting better airflow and improved drying efficiency. Both types cater to specific material characteristics and production needs, ensuring optimal drying performance for powders, granules, and other bulk substances while maintaining product quality and minimizing energy consumption.</p></p>
