<p><p>The medical cyclotron market is categorized into two main types: low energy medical cyclotrons and high energy medical cyclotrons. Low energy medical cyclotrons typically operate at energies up to 30 MeV, suitable for producing radiopharmaceuticals used in positron emission tomography (PET) imaging. In contrast, high energy medical cyclotrons operate above 30 MeV, enabling the production of a broader range of isotopes for therapies and advanced imaging techniques. Both types play critical roles in diagnostics and treatment in nuclear medicine.</p></p>
