<p><p>The ventricular assistance devices market includes three main types: Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs), Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs), and Bi-Ventricular Assist Devices (BiVADs). LVADs support the left ventricle, helping patients with heart failure by improving blood flow. RVADs assist the right ventricle, typically used in conjunction with LVADs or during specific heart conditions. BiVADs provide support for both ventricles, addressing comprehensive heart failure needs. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
