<p><p>The pump lasers market is primarily divided into two types: single-mode and multi-mode pump lasers. Single-mode pump lasers are designed for applications requiring high precision and efficiency, focusing light into a single optical fiber, often used in telecommunications and fiber optic systems. In contrast, multi-mode pump lasers are suitable for applications that can accommodate multiple light paths, providing higher output power and efficiency, commonly utilized in industrial and medical settings. Each type caters to specific operational needs and applications.</p></p>
