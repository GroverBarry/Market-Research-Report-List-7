<p><p>The medical fiber optics market is primarily divided into two types: silica (glass) fiber optics and plastic fiber optics. Silica fiber optics, made from glass, offer high durability, excellent light transmission, and resistance to environmental factors, making them ideal for various medical applications, including endoscopy and laser surgeries. In contrast, plastic fiber optics are lightweight, flexible, and more cost-effective, suitable for less demanding environments. Each type caters to specific medical needs, enhancing diagnostics and treatment methods in healthcare settings.</p></p>
