Gyroscopes detect rotational movements, providing data for precise navigation and vehicle dynamics. Combo sensors integrate both functionalities, offering enhanced performance for applications like advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and electronic stability programs. These sensors collectively enhance vehicle safety, performance, and navigation precision, driving innovation in automotive technology.</p></p>
