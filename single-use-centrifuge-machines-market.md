<p><p>The single-use centrifuge machines market comprises two main types: benchtop centrifuges and continuous flow centrifuges. Benchtop centrifuges are compact, easy-to-use devices suited for small-scale applications, typically found in laboratories for processing samples. In contrast, continuous flow centrifuges are designed for larger-scale operations, enabling the continuous separation of fluids with high efficiency. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and research, focusing on optimizing processes while maintaining product integrity.</p></p>
