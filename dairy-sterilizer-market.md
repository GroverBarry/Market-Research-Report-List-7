<p><p>The dairy sterilizer market encompasses several types, including high-temperature dairy sterilizers, dairy pasteurizers, and ultraviolet (UV) dairy sterilizers. High-temperature dairy sterilizers utilize extreme heat to eliminate pathogens, ensuring product longevity. Dairy pasteurizers apply moderate heat for a shorter duration to kill harmful microorganisms while preserving flavor and nutrients. Ultraviolet dairy sterilizers employ UV light to disinfect dairy products without heating, offering a chemical-free method for extending shelf life while maintaining quality. Each type addresses specific safety and quality demands in dairy processing.</p></p>
