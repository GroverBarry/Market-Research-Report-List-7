<p><p>Manual pipettes come in two market types: single-channel and multichannel. Single-channel pipettes are designed for dispensing and transferring liquids one at a time, making them ideal for tasks that require precision and accuracy. On the other hand, multichannel pipettes allow for simultaneous dispensing of multiple samples, improving efficiency and productivity in high-throughput applications. Both types of pipettes are essential tools in laboratories for various tasks such as PCR, ELISA, and sample preparation.</p></p>
