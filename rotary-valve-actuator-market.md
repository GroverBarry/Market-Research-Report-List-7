<p><p>The rotary valve actuator market is primarily segmented into four types: pneumatic, hydraulic, electric, and others. Pneumatic actuators utilize compressed air for operation, providing rapid response and high force. Hydraulic actuators rely on fluid pressure, offering substantial power and precision, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Electric actuators use electric motors to drive valve movement, allowing for precise control and integration with automation systems. Other types may include mechanical or manual actuators, catering to specific niche applications and requirements.</p></p>
