<p><p>The Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD) wire market primarily consists of two types: thin-film RTDs and wire-wound RTDs. Thin-film RTDs utilize a fine metal film deposited on a ceramic substrate, offering fast response times and compact designs, making them ideal for integrated applications. In contrast, wire-wound RTDs consist of a fine wire coil wrapped around a ceramic or glass core, providing high durability and accuracy, suitable for industrial applications. Both types are essential for precise temperature measurement across various sectors.</p></p>
