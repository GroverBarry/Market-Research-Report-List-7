The MDPE market is primarily segmented by production processes: the autoclave process and tubular process. The autoclave process involves the polymerization of ethylene in a large pressure vessel, resulting in higher molecular weight materials. In contrast, the tubular process employs a continuous flow method, yielding MDPE with a consistent quality and structure, catering to various applications in packaging, construction, and more.</p></p>
