<p><p>The semiconductor gate valve market is primarily categorized into two connection types: flanged and threaded connections. Flanged connection valves feature a flat, machine-faced surface and are bolted to piping systems, providing strong mechanical stability and ease of maintenance, ideal for larger systems. Conversely, threaded connection valves use screws to connect with pipes, offering a compact design and simple installation suitable for smaller applications. Both types play crucial roles in controlling fluid flow within semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
