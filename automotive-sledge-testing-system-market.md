<p><p>The Automotive Sledge Testing System market includes two primary types: Decelerator Sled Testing Systems and Catapult Sled Testing Systems. Decelerator sleds are designed to simulate crash scenarios by rapidly decelerating a vehicle, assessing its structural integrity and safety features during impacts. In contrast, catapult sleds propel vehicles at high speeds to mimic real-world collisions, enabling engineers to analyze performance and occupant safety. Both systems are essential for automotive safety testing, facilitating the development and certification of safer vehicles.</p></p>
