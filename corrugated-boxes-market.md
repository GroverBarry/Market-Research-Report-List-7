<p><p>The corrugated boxes market is divided into three main types based on the number of corrugated layers. Single corrugated boxes have one layer of corrugated medium and are lightweight, ideal for general packaging. Double corrugated boxes feature two layers of corrugated medium, offering additional strength and protection for heavy or fragile items. Triple corrugated boxes consist of three layers, providing maximum durability and resistance, making them suitable for transporting highly sensitive products or bulk items in demanding environments.</p></p>
