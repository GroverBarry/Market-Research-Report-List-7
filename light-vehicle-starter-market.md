<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle starter market can be segmented into two primary types: direct control and electromagnetic control. Direct control starters utilize a mechanical connection to engage the starter motor directly, providing a straightforward and efficient operation. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters rely on an electromagnetic mechanism to engage the starter, offering smoother operation and potentially improved reliability. Both types serve the same function of initiating the engine but differ in their engagement methods and operational characteristics, impacting performance and efficiency.</p></p>
