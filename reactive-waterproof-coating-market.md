<p><strong>The Reactive Waterproof Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Reactive Waterproof Coating</li><li>Dry Reactive Waterproof Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reactive waterproof coating market encompasses two primary types: liquid reactive waterproof coatings and dry reactive waterproof coatings. Liquid reactive coatings are applied in a fluid state, forming a robust, seamless membrane upon curing, ideal for various surfaces and environments. In contrast, dry reactive waterproof coatings come in a powdered form that activates upon mixing with water or other agents, providing excellent adhesion and flexibility. Both types effectively enhance water resistance, protecting structures from moisture damage and improving durability.</p></p>
