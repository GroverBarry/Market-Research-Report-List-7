<p><p>The reusable diapers market comprises various types designed for different needs. Pocket type diapers feature a waterproof outer layer with a pocket for absorbent inserts, allowing customization. Fitted type diapers are contoured for a snug fit, typically requiring a waterproof cover. All-in-one type diapers combine the absorbent layer and waterproof cover into a single unit for convenience. All-in-two type diapers separate the absorbent layer from the outer cover, facilitating easy washing. Other types may include prefolds, flats, and hybrid systems, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
