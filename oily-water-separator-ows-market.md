<p><p>Oily Water Separator (OWS) is a device used to treat oily water before discharging it into the environment. There are three main types of OWS systems: mechanical-based, chemical-based, and membrane-based. Mechanical-based OWS relies on physical separation of oil from water using gravity or centrifugal force. Chemical-based OWS uses chemicals to break down oil particles and separate them from water. Membrane-based OWS utilizes membranes to filter out oil and other contaminants from water. Each type offers different advantages and is suitable for various applications.</p></p>
