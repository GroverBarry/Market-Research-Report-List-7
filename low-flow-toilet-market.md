<p><p>The low-flow toilet market primarily includes siphonic toilets and wash-down toilets. Siphonic toilets utilize a curved trap design that creates a vacuum effect, effectively pulling waste away with less water, making them efficient in water usage. In contrast, wash-down toilets rely on gravity to flush waste into the bowl, requiring slightly more water but offering simpler designs and easier maintenance. Both types are designed to reduce water consumption while maintaining effective waste management in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
