<p><p>The screws and fasteners market encompasses various types, including screws, bolts, nuts, washers, and rivets. Screws are helical fasteners used to join materials, while bolts serve similar purposes but require a nut for secure fastening. Nuts are paired with bolts to provide a tight hold, and washers distribute load and reduce friction. Rivets are permanent fasteners that secure materials by deforming the rivet's end. Together, these components play a crucial role in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries, ensuring structural integrity and assembly efficiency.</p></p>
