<p><p>The flexible dies market is divided into two main types: laser hardened and not laser hardened. Laser hardened dies undergo a specialized laser treatment that enhances their durability and wear resistance, making them suitable for high-volume production and complex designs. Both types cater to various applications in packaging, labels, and printing, offering businesses choices based on budget and output needs.</p></p>
