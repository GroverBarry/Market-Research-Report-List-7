<p><p>The medical biocompatible material market encompasses various types, including PEEK (Polyetheretherketone) and PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone). PEEK is known for its high strength, chemical resistance, and temperature stability, making it ideal for implants and orthopedic devices. PPSU, on the other hand, offers excellent thermal stability and low moisture absorption, making it suitable for surgical instruments and sterilizable components. Both materials are essential in the healthcare sector, ensuring durable, safe, and effective solutions for various medical applications.</p></p>
