<p><p>The LED encapsulant market primarily comprises two types: epoxy and silicone. Epoxy encapsulants are known for their excellent adhesion, durability, and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. They offer a rigid protective layer that enhances light output and lifespan. Conversely, silicone encapsulants provide superior flexibility, UV resistance, and thermal management, making them ideal for applications exposed to varying environmental conditions. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing LED performance and longevity within various lighting applications.</p></p>
