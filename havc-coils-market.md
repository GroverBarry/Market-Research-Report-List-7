<p><p>The HVAC coils market comprises two main types: evaporator coils and condenser coils. Evaporator coils are essential for heat absorption in cooling systems, facilitating the conversion of refrigerant from liquid to gas, thereby cooling the air. In contrast, condenser coils are responsible for releasing heat, enabling the refrigerant to revert from gas to liquid. Both coil types are crucial in HVAC systems, contributing to energy efficiency and temperature regulation in residential and commercial environments.</p></p>
