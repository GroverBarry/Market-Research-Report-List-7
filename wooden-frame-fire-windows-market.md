<p><p>The wooden frame fire windows market encompasses two main types: fixed fire windows and movable fire windows. Fixed fire windows are designed to remain in place during a fire, providing resistance to flames and smoke while maintaining structural integrity. Movable fire windows, on the other hand, can be opened or closed to facilitate ventilation while still offering fire protection. Both types are crucial for enhancing safety in buildings, conforming to fire regulations, and ensuring occupant safety in fire emergencies.</p></p>
