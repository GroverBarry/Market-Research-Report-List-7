<p><p>Safety PLC (Programmable Logic Controller) is a type of PLC that is specifically designed to ensure the safe operation of machinery and equipment in industrial settings. These features help to prevent accidents and minimize the risk of injury to workers.</p><p>The Safety PLC market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing emphasis on workplace safety and the implementation of stringent safety regulations in industries such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and automotive.
