<p><ul><li>LCD Projector</li><li>DLP Projector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceiling-mounted projector market primarily features two types: LCD and DLP projectors. LCD projectors use liquid crystal displays to produce bright, vibrant images, making them ideal for environments with ambient light. They offer excellent color accuracy but can be bulkier. DLP projectors utilize a digital light processing chip, which delivers sharper images and smoother video playback. Known for their compact design and reliability, DLP projectors are preferred for home theaters and business presentations, offering versatility and ease of installation.</p></p>
