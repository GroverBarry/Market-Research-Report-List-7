<p><p>The organic cocoa market comprises three main product types: cocoa liquor, cocoa powder, and cocoa butter. Cocoa liquor, a blend of cocoa solids and cocoa butter, serves as a base for chocolate production. Cocoa powder, derived from roasted cocoa beans, is used in baking and beverages for its rich flavor and health benefits. Cocoa butter, extracted from the fat of cocoa beans, is essential for creating smooth chocolate and is also valued in cosmetics for its moisturizing properties.</p></p>
