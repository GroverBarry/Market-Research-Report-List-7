Piezo-electric Sensor Wires utilize changes in pressure and mechanical stress to measure flow rates in biological systems, offering high sensitivity and real-time data. Pressure Microcatheters, on the other hand, are designed for precise pressure measurements in small vessels, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in clinical settings. Both technologies play a crucial role in advancing medical diagnostics and treatment monitoring, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
