<p><p>Natural graphite is categorized into two main types for lithium battery applications: crystalline graphite and implicit crystalline graphite. </p><p>Crystalline graphite, characterized by its highly ordered atomic structure, offers superior electrochemical performance due to better conductivity and stability, making it ideal for anodes in lithium-ion batteries. </p><p>Implicit crystalline graphite, while less organized, serves as a cost-effective alternative, often providing satisfactory performance at a lower price point. Both types are essential for meeting the growing demand for efficient energy storage solutions.</p></p>
