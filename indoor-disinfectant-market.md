<p><p>The indoor disinfectant market is primarily segmented into two types: non-oxidizing disinfectants and oxidizing disinfectants. Non-oxidizing disinfectants, such as quaternary ammonium compounds, work by disrupting microbial cell membranes, effectively killing bacteria and viruses. In contrast, oxidizing disinfectants, like hydrogen peroxide and sodium hypochlorite, utilize oxidation reactions to destroy pathogens. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring hygienic environments in residential, commercial, and healthcare settings, catering to diverse consumer needs for effective disinfection solutions.</p></p>
