<p><p>The medical suction regulators market is primarily divided into two main types: continuous suction regulators and intermittent suction regulators. Continuous suction regulators provide a steady flow of suction, ideal for procedures requiring constant drainage of fluids, such as in surgeries or emergencies. In contrast, intermittent suction regulators allow for variable suction levels, enabling controlled and periodic fluid removal, which is beneficial in situations like wound care or in patients needing occasional suction assistance. Both types are essential for effective medical interventions.</p></p>
