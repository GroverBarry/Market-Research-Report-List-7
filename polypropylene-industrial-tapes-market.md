<p><p>The Polypropylene Industrial Tapes market comprises two main types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for applications like mounting, sealing, and insulation. It offers easy application and versatility across various surfaces. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, providing a strong bond between two surfaces without visible tape. This type is commonly used for bonding, crafting, and automotive applications, offering a clean finish and discrete adhesion for varied industrial needs.</p></p>
