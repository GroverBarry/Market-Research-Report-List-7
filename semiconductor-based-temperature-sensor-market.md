<p><p>The semiconductor-based temperature sensor market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Voltage Output Temperature Sensors** produce a voltage proportional to temperature, ideal for simple applications. </p><p>2. **Current Output Temperature Sensors** transmit temperature data as a current signal, beneficial for long-distance transmission with reduced noise. </p><p>3. **Digital Output Temperature Sensors** deliver temperature readings in digital formats for precise and easy integration. </p><p>4. **Resistance Output Silicon Temperature Sensors** utilize changing resistance with temperature for accurate readings. </p><p>5. **Diode Temperature Sensors** exploit forward voltage drop variations across diodes to measure temperature efficiently.</p></p>
