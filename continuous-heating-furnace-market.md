<p><p>The Continuous Heating Furnace market features various types, including Rotary-Hearth and Straight-Chamber furnaces. Rotary-Hearth Type furnaces utilize a rotating platform to evenly distribute heat, enhancing efficiency for high-volume production. In contrast, Straight-Chamber Type furnaces offer a linear design, allowing processes like annealing and sintering in a straightforward pathway. Both types are crucial for industries requiring consistent heating processes, such as metalworking and ceramics, addressing different needs in terms of capacity, energy efficiency, and temperature control.</p></p>
