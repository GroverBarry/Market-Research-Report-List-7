<p><ul><li>Rubber Bellows Type</li><li>Sleeve-Style Type</li><li>Coil Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air bag suspension systems come in three main types: rubber bellows, sleeve-style, and coil. Rubber bellows type air bags are made from layers of rubber and fabric, offering a smooth ride and durability. Sleeve-style air bags have a fabric sleeve surrounding the air bag, providing added protection. Coil type air bags, on the other hand, feature a spring-like coil inside the air bag for increased support and stability. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market for air bag suspension systems.</p></p>
