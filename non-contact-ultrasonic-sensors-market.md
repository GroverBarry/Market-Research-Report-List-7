<p><p>The non-contact ultrasonic sensors market is divided into two main types: open type and sealed type. Open type sensors are designed for environments where moisture, dust, or particulates are minimal, allowing for direct exposure to the surrounding air. In contrast, sealed type sensors are encapsulated to protect against harsh conditions, making them suitable for industrial applications where contaminants may be present. Both types facilitate distance measurement and object detection without physical contact, enhancing safety and accuracy in various applications.</p></p>
