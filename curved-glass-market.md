<p><p>The curved glass market encompasses several types including annealed curved glass, laminated curved glass, and double glazed curved glass. Annealed curved glass is heat-treated for strength but lacks added safety features. Laminated curved glass combines layers of glass with an interlayer, enhancing safety and noise reduction. Double glazed curved glass features two glass layers with an air gap, improving insulation and energy efficiency. Each type serves distinct applications across architecture, automotive, and design, tailored to specific structural and aesthetic needs.</p></p>
