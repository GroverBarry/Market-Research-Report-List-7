<p><strong>The Pneumatic Tourniquet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Bladder Tourniquet</li><li>Dual Bladder Tourniquet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic tourniquet market comprises primarily two types: single bladder and dual bladder tourniquets. Single bladder tourniquets utilize one inflatable chamber to constrict blood flow during surgical procedures, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, dual bladder tourniquets feature two inflatable chambers that can be independently controlled, providing enhanced control and stability in managing blood circulation. Both types are essential in surgical settings to minimize bleeding and improve procedural outcomes, catering to varying clinician preferences and surgical requirements.</p></p>
