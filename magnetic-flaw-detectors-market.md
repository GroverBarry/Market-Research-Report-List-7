Magnetic Yokes and Portable Units are versatile, allowing for on-site inspection of welds and metal components by generating a magnetic field to reveal surface and subsurface flaws. Magnetic Benches, on the other hand, are stationary, providing a fixed platform for detailed inspections, ideal for larger components in controlled environments. Both types enhance the efficiency and accuracy of non-destructive testing in various industries.</p></p>
