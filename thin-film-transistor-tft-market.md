<p><p>TFT market can be categorized into two main types: Organic and Inorganic. Organic TFTs are made using carbon-based materials, offering flexibility and low-cost production. They are commonly used in applications such as wearable devices and flexible displays. On the other hand, Inorganic TFTs are made using materials like silicon, offering higher performance and stability compared to organic TFTs. They are commonly used in high-resolution displays and advanced electronic devices. Both types of TFTs have their own advantages and are used in various industries based on specific requirements.</p></p>
