<p><p>The automotive grade LiDAR scanner market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical LiDAR and solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR utilizes rotating components to scan the environment, offering high resolution and a wide field of view but often at a higher cost and complexity. In contrast, solid-state LiDAR technologies are compact, with fewer moving parts, leading to greater durability and lower manufacturing costs, making them suitable for mass production in autonomous vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicular safety and navigation.</p></p>
