There are two main types in the market - Single Rotor Rotary Rakes and Multi-Rotor Rotary Rakes. Single rotor rakes have one spinning rotor while multi-rotor rakes have multiple rotors that work together to gather and form windrows. Single rotor rakes are more compact and maneuverable, making them suitable for smaller fields, while multi-rotor rakes are more productive and efficient in larger fields.</p></p>
