<p><p>The Acute Ischemic Stroke Therapeutics market encompasses several key therapeutic types. Thrombolytic therapeutics aim to dissolve blood clots quickly, improving blood flow to the brain. Antihypertensive therapeutics manage high blood pressure, reducing stroke risk. Antiplatelet therapeutics prevent blood cell aggregation, thereby minimizing clot formation. Anticoagulants inhibit blood coagulation, ensuring better blood flow and reducing the likelihood of future strokes. Together, these therapies are crucial for effective management and treatment of acute ischemic strokes, optimizing patient outcomes and survival rates.</p></p>
