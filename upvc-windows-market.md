<p><p>The uPVC windows market is categorized into single glazing, double glazing, and triple glazing. Single glazing features a single pane of glass, offering basic insulation but limited energy efficiency. Double glazing incorporates two panes, providing improved thermal insulation and noise reduction, making it a popular choice for residential properties. Triple glazing adds a third pane, maximizing energy efficiency and soundproofing, ideal for areas with extreme weather. Each type caters to different needs, balancing cost and performance for consumers.</p></p>
