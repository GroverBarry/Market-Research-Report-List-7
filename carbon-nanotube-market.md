<p><strong>The Carbon Nanotube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT)</li><li>Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT)</li><li>Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon nanotube market is categorized into three main types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT), and Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT). SWNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms rolled into a tube, offering exceptional electrical and mechanical properties. MWNTs contain multiple layers of carbon, enhancing strength and conductivity. DWNTs fall between the two, featuring two concentric tubes, providing a balance of properties. Each type has distinct applications in electronics, materials science, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
