<p><p>The high-performance glass fiber market is categorized into two main types: polymer composites and non-polymer composites. Polymer composites involve glass fibers embedded in polymer matrices, offering excellent strength, lightweight properties, and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. Non-polymer composites, on the other hand, utilize glass fibers with inorganic matrices, providing superior thermal and mechanical properties, often used in construction, marine, and high-temperature applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
