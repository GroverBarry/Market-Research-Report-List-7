<p><p>The composite coatings market encompasses several methods for enhancing material properties. Laser melt injection involves melting a substrate surface with a laser to bond additional materials, improving wear and corrosion resistance. Brazing is a process of joining materials by melting a filler metal, ensuring strong connections in various applications. Electroless plating applies a coating through a chemical reduction process, resulting in uniform layers without electrical current. Other techniques include thermal spraying and physical vapor deposition, broadening the market's scope.</p></p>
