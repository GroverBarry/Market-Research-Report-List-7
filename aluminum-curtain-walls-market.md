<p><ul><li>Stick-built</li><li>Semi-unitized</li><li>Unitized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum curtain walls are commonly categorized into three main types based on their installation methods: stick-built, semi-unitized, and unitized. Stick-built curtain walls involve assembling on-site, piece by piece, offering flexibility in design but requiring more time for installation. Semi-unitized curtain walls are partially assembled in the factory and finished on-site, offering a balance between cost and efficiency. Unitized curtain walls are pre-fabricated modules that are fully assembled in the factory and installed on-site, providing the quickest installation but limiting design flexibility.</p></p>
