<p><p>Medical Practice Management Software (PMS) facilitates administrative and clinical tasks in healthcare settings. The market types include Web-based, On-premise, and Cloud-based solutions. Web-based PMS is accessed via the internet, enabling remote access with minimal local installation. On-premise software requires installation on local servers, offering greater control but demanding more maintenance. Cloud-based PMS stores data on remote servers, ensuring automatic updates and improved scalability while enhancing collaboration among healthcare providers. Each type caters to specific operational needs and preferences of medical practices.</p></p>
