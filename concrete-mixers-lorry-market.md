Dry type mixers, on the other hand, carry raw materials separately, mixing them at the site for fresh concrete. Other market types may include specialty mixers designed for specific applications, such as self-loading or volumetric mixers, which provide flexibility and adaptability for various construction needs. Each type serves unique operational requirements.</p></p>
