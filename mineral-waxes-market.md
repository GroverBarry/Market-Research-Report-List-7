<p><p>The mineral waxes market comprises various types, including peat waxes, ozocerite, montan wax, and ceresin waxes. Peat waxes, derived from decomposed plant material, are primarily used in cosmetics and candles. Ozocerite, a natural mineral, is known for its high melting point and is utilized in industrial applications. Montan wax, sourced from lignite, serves in rubber and plastics, while ceresin wax, a refined form of ozocerite, is valued for its smooth texture and stability in diverse products.</p></p>
