<p><p>Metal Forming Equipment Market Types consist of Bulk Forming, Sheet Forming, and Powder Metal Forming. Bulk Forming involves shaping metal into desired forms through processes like forging, extrusion, and rolling. Sheet Forming bends and reshapes metal sheets using techniques like stamping and deep drawing. Powder Metal Forming creates metal parts from powder through processes like sintering and metal injection molding. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs and applications in various industries, contributing to the versatility of metal forming equipment in the market.</p></p>
