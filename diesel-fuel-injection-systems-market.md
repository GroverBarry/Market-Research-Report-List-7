<p><ul><li>Pump-Line-Nozzle Injection System</li><li>Common Rail Injection System</li><li>Other Injection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel fuel injection systems market encompasses several types, primarily the Pump-Line-Nozzle system, Common Rail Injection system, and other injection systems. </p><p>The Pump-Line-Nozzle system features a high-pressure pump that delivers fuel to individual nozzles, ensuring precise timing and atomization. The Common Rail Injection system centralizes fuel delivery, allowing multiple injectors to draw from a shared high-pressure rail, enhancing efficiency and emissions control. Other injection systems include various innovations aimed at improving performance and compatibility, catering to diverse diesel engine applications.</p></p>
