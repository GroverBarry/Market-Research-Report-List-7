<p><ul><li>Non-reactive Type</li><li>Reactive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin diluent and modifier market is categorized into two main types: non-reactive and reactive types. Non-reactive diluents are typically solvents that reduce viscosity and improve processing without participating in the curing reaction, enhancing the flow and application of resins. In contrast, reactive diluents chemically interact with the resin during the curing process, contributing to the final properties of the material. Both types serve to optimize resin formulations for various applications, including coatings, adhesives, and composites.</p></p>
