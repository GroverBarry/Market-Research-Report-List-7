<p><strong>The Dielectric Resonator For 5G Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEM Mode</li><li>TE Mode</li><li>TM Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dielectric resonators for the 5G market utilize different electromagnetic modes: TEM, TE, and TM modes. TEM (Transverse Electromagnetic) mode supports both electric and magnetic fields perpendicular to the direction of propagation, ideal for low-frequency applications. TE (Transverse Electric) mode has an electric field solely transverse to the direction of propagation, suitable for higher frequency and low-loss applications. TM (Transverse Magnetic) mode features a magnetic field perpendicular to the propagation direction, enabling effective waveguiding and filtering in advanced 5G systems.</p></p>
