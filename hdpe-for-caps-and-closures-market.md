<p><p>HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is widely used in the caps and closures market due to its strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals. Two primary manufacturing processes for HDPE caps and closures are injection molding and compression molding. Injection molding involves injecting molten HDPE into a mold, allowing for precise shapes and fast production. Compression molding, on the other hand, involves placing HDPE in a heated mold where it is compressed to form the desired shape, offering flexibility in design and material utilization.</p></p>
