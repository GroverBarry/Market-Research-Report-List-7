<p><p>Hot-melt adhesives are categorized into several types based on their polymer structures. Thermoplastic polyurethane offers high flexibility and durability, ideal for demanding applications. Ethylene vinyl acetate provides excellent adhesion and is commonly used in packaging. Styrenic-butadiene copolymers are valued for their flexibility and strength, making them suitable for a variety of substrates. Polyolefins are noted for their chemical resistance and are often used in automotive applications. Polyamide adhesives offer high-temperature resistance and are utilized in the electronics and textiles industries.</p></p>
