<p><p>The Transformer Monitoring Software market encompasses various specialized tools designed to enhance transformer performance and safety. DGA (Dissolved Gas Analysis) software analyzes gas byproducts to detect potential insulation failures. Bushing Monitoring Software focuses on the health of transformer bushings, crucial for preventing failures. Partial Discharge (PD) Software identifies and analyzes electrical discharges within insulation, indicating deterioration. The "Others" segment includes additional monitoring tools, such as thermal monitoring and vibration analysis, providing a comprehensive approach to transformer health management.</p></p>
